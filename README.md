# Legal Team Starter

**Founders doing their own legal: draft ToS, flag risky contract clauses, map compliance.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

Reach for this when you are a founder or lean in-house team handling legal work without a firm on retainer. It gets you to a solid first draft of your SaaS terms of service and privacy policy, flags the non-standard clauses (non-competes, IP assignment, severance) hiding in an employment agreement before you sign, maps which regulations apply to your product and where the gaps are, and verifies factual claims against independent sources when you need to be sure. The legal skills are explicit that they produce strong drafts and triage, not legal advice — use them to know what to fix and when to bring in a lawyer.

## Install

- **From the catalog:** [skillme.dev/pack/legal-team-starter](https://skillme.dev/pack/legal-team-starter) — install the whole pack into Claude in one step.
- **With the skills CLI:** `npx skills add aouellets/legal-team-starter`
- **Manually:** copy any `skills/<slug>/SKILL.md` into your Claude skills directory.

## Skills in this pack

- **[Regulatory Scanner](skills/regulatory-scan/SKILL.md)** — Maps relevant regulations for a product or industry with compliance requirements and gaps.
- **[Terms of Service](skills/terms-of-service/SKILL.md)** — Drafts plain-English terms of service and privacy policies for SaaS products.
- **[Employment Contract Review](skills/employment-contract/SKILL.md)** — Flags non-standard clauses in employment contracts: non-competes, IP assignment, severance.
